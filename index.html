Behold, Ardor!

For most species on the planet, this world is yet a young one.

The sentient races still wander the land in great migratory tribes and have not yet chosen a land to call home.

The beasts of the field have not yet been taught to obey and the wild grains of the vast prairies have not yet been confined to the fields.

There are no grand kingdoms or great orders of wizards and no organized sects of clergy.

Darkness and viciousness are in no small supply as hosts of monsters yet rule the realm and have not yet retreated to their mountain peaks and otherwise dark places.
